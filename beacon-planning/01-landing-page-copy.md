## How It Works Section

**Section label:** Three steps. Two minutes.

**Step 1:**
**Import your clients**
Upload a CSV from your AMS — EZLynx, HawkSoft, or any system that exports to spreadsheet. Beacon maps your columns automatically and adds every client to your renewal calendar. Takes about 3 minutes for your full book of business.

**Step 2:**
**Generate the letter**
Open a client from your renewal calendar. Pick the scenario: Pre-Renewal Outreach, Rate Increase Explanation, or New Client Welcome. Beacon pre-fills the letter with their details — name, policy type, renewal date, carrier, premium. Click Generate. Your letter appears in seconds.

**Step 3:**
**Edit, then send from your email**
Review the letter in the built-in editor. Make any changes you want. When you're happy, click Send — the letter goes directly from your Gmail or Outlook to your client. Not from a Beacon address. From you.

Beacon logs every letter you generate and send, automatically, forever. Your E&O documentation happens without you lifting a finger.

## FAQ Section

**Section label:** Questions we get asked

**Q: Do the letters go out from a Beacon email address?**
No. When you connect your Gmail or Outlook account, letters send from your personal email address. Your client sees your name and your email — not Beacon's.

**Q: What if I don't want to connect my email?**
No problem. Every letter can be downloaded as a PDF or copied to your clipboard. Email send is optional. You can draft letters in Beacon and send them however you prefer.

**Q: What kind of letters does Beacon write?**
At launch, Beacon handles three scenarios: Pre-Renewal Outreach (reaching out before a renewal conversation), Rate Increase Explanation (explaining a premium increase without losing the client), and New Client Welcome (onboarding a new policyholder with a personal note). More scenarios are in development.

**Q: Do I have to import all my clients?**
No. You can add clients one at a time manually, or you can skip client import entirely and just type in a client's details when you generate a letter. CSV import is the fastest way to get your full book into Beacon, but it's not required.

**Q: Does Beacon keep my E&O records automatically?**
Yes. Every letter you generate is logged automatically — even if you don't send it. The log includes the date, time, client name, scenario, and the full letter text. It's tamper-proof (no one can edit or delete log entries, including you) and exportable as a PDF whenever your E&O carrier needs documentation.

**Q: Is Beacon GLBA compliant?**
Yes. Beacon is designed to meet the requirements of the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act Safeguards Rule. We have a Written Information Security Program, a Data Processing Agreement available for agencies that need one, and all client data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**
You keep access to your E&O log and client list until the end of your billing period. After that, your account is downgraded to the free tier. We retain your data for 30 days after account deletion so you can export anything you need. Your E&O log is retained for 7 years regardless, in case you need it for a future audit.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes. Month-to-month, no contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel and you keep access until the end of the month you've already paid for.

**Q: What's included in the free trial?**
10 letter generations. No credit card required. Full access to all Solo plan features — import clients, connect your email, generate letters, view your renewal calendar. When your 10 letters are used, you're prompted to upgrade.

**Q: Does Beacon work with my AMS?**
Beacon imports clients via CSV file, which every major AMS can export (EZLynx, HawkSoft, Applied Epic, AMS360, and others). A direct AMS integration is planned for a future release.

## Billing FAQ (Pricing Page)

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes. Every new account starts with 10 free letter generations. No credit card required. When your trial letters are used, you're prompted to choose a plan.

**Do I pay per agent on my team?**
No. Agency and Office plans are flat-rate. Add 2 producers or 5 — same price. You pay for the plan, not the headcount.

**What counts as a "letter generation"?**
Every time you click Generate and Beacon produces a letter, that's one generation. Editing, saving, sending, and re-sending do not count.

**Can I switch plans?**
Yes, anytime. Upgrade immediately (new features available right away). Downgrade takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

**Do you offer annual billing?**
Not at launch. Monthly billing only. Annual pricing with a discount is planned for a future release.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) through Stripe. No PayPal or ACH at launch.

**What happens if my payment fails?**
You have a 3-day grace period. You'll receive an email and an in-app notification to update your payment method. After 3 days, your account is downgraded to the free tier (10-letter limit). All your data stays safe.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes. Cancel in your billing settings in under a minute. You keep full access until the end of the period you've paid for. No fees, no penalties.

**Is there a contract?**
No. Month-to-month. Cancel whenever you want.

**Do you offer refunds?**
We don't offer refunds for partial months. If you cancel mid-month, you keep access through the end of that billing period. Contact support@get-monolith.com if you have an unusual situation.

**Do you offer a discount for larger agencies?**
If you need more than 15 agents (Office tier), contact us at support@get-monolith.com and we'll put together custom pricing.

**Is Beacon GLBA compliant? Can I get a DPA?**
Yes. Contact legal@get-monolith.com to request a Data Processing Agreement for your records.
