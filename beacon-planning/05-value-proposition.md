# Beacon — Value Proposition

## The One-Sentence Value Proposition

**Beacon helps independent insurance agents retain more clients by writing every proactive letter they know they should be sending — renewal outreach, rate increase explanations, new client welcomes — in their own voice, in 60 seconds.**

---

## Expanded Value Proposition (for internal clarity)

**For:** Independent insurance agents and small agencies

**Who have:** A retention problem caused by inconsistent, time-consuming client communication

**Beacon is:** An AI-powered client communication platform

**That:** Generates personalized, professional letters for every client situation in the agent's own voice, sends them directly from the agent's email, and logs every communication for E&O compliance

**Unlike:** Generic AI tools (ChatGPT), expensive enterprise platforms (Zywave), or manual Word templates

**Beacon's key differentiator is:** Insurance-specific AI that knows what to say, when to say it, and how to say it in the agent's voice — with one-click send from their own Gmail or Outlook and automatic E&O documentation built in

---

## The Core Insight

The #1 reason independent insurance agents lose clients is not bad products, not price, and not competition from direct carriers.

It's silence.

65% of clients who leave never spoke to their agent before canceling. They got a renewal notice in the mail, the number was higher than last year, and they went to Google. If their agent had called or written to them 90 days earlier — explained the market, showed they were watching — 80% of those clients would have stayed.

Agents know this. Every agent knows they should be writing pre-renewal letters, rate increase explanations, and annual review invitations. They don't do it consistently because writing 40 personalized letters every month is 20+ hours of unbillable work.

Beacon removes that constraint entirely.

## The Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

**"The only tool built specifically to stop insurance agents from losing clients to silence."**

Supporting proof points:
1. Insurance-specific AI — knows policy types, carrier language, rate increase reasons, E&O implications. Not a generic writing tool.
2. Agent voice learning — letters sound like the agent wrote them, not like an AI did.
3. Gmail/Outlook send — one click from letter to delivered. No copy-paste, no switching apps.
4. E&O documentation log — every communication stored, timestamped, exportable. Built-in legal protection.
5. Renewal calendar — shows exactly who needs outreach today. Turns reactive agents into proactive ones.
6. Price accessible to solo agents — $149/month, not $2,400-40,000/year like Zywave.
